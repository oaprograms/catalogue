Enabling notifications

Follow the steps below to add/allow LLN Flashcards in Google Chrome, so that Push Notifications are allowed:

 

On Android

  1. Open Chrome.

  2. At the top right, click More  > Settings.

  3. Click Notifications.

  4. Ensure that the toggle next to Show Notifications is enabled.

  5. Scroll down and ensure that https://cards.dioco.io is enabled.

On PC

  1. Open Chrome.

  2. At the top right, click More  > Settings.

  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the Settings page and then click Advanced.

  4. Under "Privacy and security," click Site settings.

  5. Click Notifications.

  6. Confirm that https://cards.dioco.io appears under "Allowed" and is not "Blocked." If it is "Blocked," click More  and click Allow.

  7. If it is not in the Allowed or Blocked list, click Add, enter the PWA URL, and then click Add.
